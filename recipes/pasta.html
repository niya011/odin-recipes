Spicy Red Sauce Pasta Recipe!

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes! Pasta is a very versatile dish, and there are many ways to make it. This recipe I'm sharing today blends the flavors of tomato, red chilis, and mozzerella, to provide a tasty, spicy, and cheesy pasta dish.

Ingredients:

  • Penne Pasta
  • Water
  • Red Pasta Sauce
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Red Chili Flakes (or powder)
  • Chopped Onions
  • Schezwan Sauce
  • Sour Cream
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil your pasta for 11-15 minutes. Drain the Water and let the pasta sit while you make the sauce.
  2. Pour in the pasta sauce to let it heat, and chop a full onion while it heats up.
  3. In the pasta sauce, add in your schezwan sauce, salt, and red chili. (You can add some black pepper too, but it's not required.)
  4. Add the sour cream last, and make sure the heat is turned down low when you do it. Mix everything well.
  5. When everything is mixed thouroughly, add your boiled pasta to the sauce. Keep the heat low and stir for a few minutes to heat up the pasta and make sure the sauce coats it all.
  6. Plate the pasta, and add cheese to your liking. Enjoy!
