Egg Toast Recipe!

Description

On most days, there is one thing I like to eat every morning without fail. Eggs. Recently, I've been obsessed with eggs on toast and thought I would share the recipe. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs (however many you want to eat)
  • Bread
  • Salt
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Sandwich Spread
  • Hot Sauce

Steps:

  1. Put your bread in the toaster and let it toast while you make the eggs.
  2. In a warm and oiled pan, break your eggs carefully so the yolks don't crack.
  3. While the eggs cook on one side, add your salt, red chili, and black pepper to taste.
  4. Once you think the eggs have cooked on one side, flip them over and let them cook more. While this happens, take your toasted bread and apply your sandwich spread to each toast.
  5. Plate the individual eggs on each toast and add cheese on top if you want. Sprinkle some hot sauce on top of the eggs as well.
